The Front End Drupal Gathering

A quasi-official DrupalCon event

New Orleans Convention Center - Room 275

9am - 5pm, May 9, 2016

Join fellow front-end developers, themers, designers, UX practitioners and more for a day-long working session at the New Orleans Convention Center, Monday before DrupalCon.

We'll discuss, debate, share, sketch and hopefully achieve consensus as we dive into the following topics:

  • Working with Twig and the D8 Theming Experience
  • Building decoupled JS-powered front ends
  • Evolving Drupal's component-based future
  • Improving Drupal's admin UX
  • Other stuff as may come up

The front end landscape has never been so wide open. If you've ever wanted to help shape its front end direction, you should come to this event.

A working session for contributors to collaborate

This is not a training or a series of presentations. It is a day-long working session for people looking to evolve and move Front End Drupal forward. If you are looking for hands-on training, or looking to attend speaker talks, please consider one of the many Drupalcon trainings available.

Sounds great! How do I join?

Sign up on our registration form. That's it!

You do not need a DrupalCon ticket to attend this summit.

This event adheres to both the Drupal Code of Conduct as well as the event-specific DrupalCon Code of Conduct.
